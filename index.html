About

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the WVU Regional Research Institute and a regional economist. My research focuses on how local labor markets adjust to structural economic changes, with a goal of providing policy-relevant insights to inform workforce development policies in rural and distressed areas.

My work spans labor markets, industrial transitions, human capital, and regional growth.

Research Interests

  • Local and regional labor markets
  • Structural economic change and industrial transitions
  • Workforce development policy in rural and distressed areas
  • Human capital and regional growth
