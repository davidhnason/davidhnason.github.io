About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Regional Research Institute at West Virginia University. I hold a Ph.D. in Natural Resource Economics from West Virginia University (2025), with fields in Urban & Regional Economics and Natural Resource & Environmental Economics.

My research focuses on how local labor markets adjust to structural economic changes, with a goal of providing policy-relevant insights to inform workforce development in rural and distressed areas. My work spans labor markets, industrial transitions, human capital, and regional growth.
